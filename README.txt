Functionalities:

- Press 1 to draw lines. Click on any two points on the app window and a line connecting them should be drawn.

- Press 2 to draw circles. Click on a point to define the origin and another one to define the radius spawn from the origin.
	- Press 3 to draw them filled with color.

- Press 4 to draw triangles. Click on any three points on the app window to draw a triangle whose nodes are on the defined points.
	- Press 5 to draw them filled with color.

Note: everything is always saved. However, pressing the space key will restart the framebuffer. Make sure to press the number of the desired mode afterwards in order to continue using the app.
